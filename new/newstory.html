Small Business Advocate of the Year

Lynne Davidson, Vice President of Tito's Tacos was awarded Small Business Advocate of the Year by the California Chamber of Commerce. She recieved this prestigious award for her outstanding contributions for community service. Lynne currently serves as a member of the board of the California Restaurant Association, and currently serves on the Policy Advisory Committee of the California Coalition on Workers' Compensation. Lynne has also served as Chairman of the Culver City Chamber of Commerce, and served as Assistant Treasurer of the Los Angeles County Republican Party.
