Thank-you Titos!

When moving to California in 1967at age 15- the first taco that I ever had in my life was a Titos taco(at the little stand on corner of Sepulveda)- and have been going there almost weekly ever since-
No wonder my kids love Titos tacos-  that is all that I craved during each of my four pregnancies.  And now, my daughter has repeated history and my three grandsons love Titos also!  And the baby granddaughter (8 mo.) loves the beans and rice.

We never complain about the long lines that we have to wait in- because it is definitely well worth it.  But, the best time to go is 9 a.m. when you first open.  And anytime is time for delicious Titos tacos, burritos and the fantastic salsa!!!!

Keep up the good cooking and thank-you again for making Titos my favorite place to eat!

Diane Hernandez
West Los Angeles
